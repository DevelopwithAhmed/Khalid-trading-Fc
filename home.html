Our Global Operations

With three strategically located branches, we have access to key markets in South Asia, the Middle East, and Central Asia, allowing us to facilitate smooth trade between producers and buyers worldwide.

We specialize in sourcing fresh fruits and vegetables, including mangoes, citrus fruits, apples, potatoes, and onions, along with premium halal meats. We also work with local artisans to export finely crafted, durable furniture.

Our Oman branch serves as a vital hub, ensuring efficient distribution of perishable goods using state-of-the-art storage and transport systems. It also supports trade in luxury and commercial furniture.

We are expanding trade by importing and distributing furniture while exporting locally grown produce and high-quality meats to new international markets.
